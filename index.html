AI

I am also an early adopter of AI - and a firm believer that it is:

  • one of the most useful tools humanity has invented, hailed as “the great equalizer”
  • overused, overblown, and potentially limiting human thinking and flourishing

As such, because I love transparency, although it probably hasn’t yet reached the point where we will value output made by humans higher than output by itself, I will explicitly state when I use AI.

As such, you have my word - as much as it is worth - that what is produced here is my own.

I ofcourse employ the use of AI - less than I should, for learning and correction.

But not for generating quick bites of beautifully cohorent but frustratingly nonsensical AI text or code.
