Critique of Health economic model assumptions using real-world data

Title Summary
Health economic model assumptions of pharmaceutical treatment paths compared with real-world evidence for patients with type 2 diabetes: A nationwide cohort study

Using registry cohort from a previous study, we examined treatment regimen and time to insulin, by starting drug, and compared the findings with assumptions used in health economic models.
